Company Overview

Liberia Renaissance Group (LRG Inc.) began as a sole proprietorship in 2013 and was incorporated in 2014. Formerly known as Liberia Renaissance Construction Company, LRG has evolved into a **full-service engineering firm** specializing in architecture, civil works, and project supervision. Our commitment is to deliver high-quality, impactful projects that contribute directly to national development. Our headquarters is located on 9th Street, Sinkor, Monrovia.

President & CEO Profile

George Delino Bryant,Sr

President / CEO

Civil Engineer, EMBA

About Mr. Bryant:

Mr. Bryant is an **Infrastructure Engineer** and **Procurement and Supply Chain expert** with 14 years of comprehensive work experience.

His expertise has been leveraged by various international organizations, including the United Nations Development Program Liberia, UN-HABITAT, UNMI, ODEBRECTH, GIZ, and Booz Allen Hamilton (BAH USA).

He possesses extensive experience in project management for multi-faceted construction projects, including detailed engineering, highway feasibility studies, maintenance, and construction. This also encompasses structural design, detailing, preparation of bills of engineering and evaluation, and the construction of bridges and buildings.

In the procurement domain, he is experienced in the preparation of annual procurement plans, budget forecasting for goods, services, and works, hiring and sourcing of consultant services, and the planning, controlling, and management of procurement activities.

Mr. Bryant further enhanced his professional qualifications by attending the **Senior Road Executive Programme at Birmingham University** in 2015 and 2018. He has achieved professional certifications in Road Maintenance Management, Road Sector Reform and Financing, Road Services Procurement and Integrity, and Road Safety, Rural Road, and Urban Planning.

He is a **Registered Engineer** and a former Executive Member of the Engineering Society of Liberia.
